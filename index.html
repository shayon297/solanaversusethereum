Real Economic Value (REV)

Solana captures similar revenue to Ethereum while keeping costs 1000x lower for users

90-Day Rolling Average

Definition: REV refers to Real Economic Value, or the total amount users are willing to pay to transact on a blockchain during a given period.

Methodology: Chain Fees + MEV Tips through Jito for Solana; Chain REV for Ethereum

Average Transaction Cost

Solana makes blockchain accessible to everyone with fees 1000x cheaper than Ethereum

Definition: Total transaction fees divided by total transaction count during a given day (including unsuccessful transactions).

Methodology: Daily transaction fees divided by daily transaction count for each blockchain

Source: TokenTerminal

Decentralized Exchange Volume

Solana DEX volume dominates Ethereum despite being years younger, proving superior user experience

90-Day Rolling Average

Definition: Total trading volume across all decentralized exchanges on each blockchain during a given period.

Methodology: Aggregated volume from all DEX protocols including Raydium, Orca, Jupiter (Solana) and Uniswap, Curve, SushiSwap (Ethereum)

Source: DefiLlama

Daily Active Addresses

Solana's user base dwarfs Ethereum with 4x more daily active addresses proving real adoption

Definition: The number of unique addresses that initiated at least one transaction on each blockchain during a given day.

Methodology: Unique address count from all on-chain transactions including successful and failed transactions

Source: DefiLlama

Number of Transactions

Methodology: Complete transaction count including all transaction types, smart contract interactions, and token transfers
